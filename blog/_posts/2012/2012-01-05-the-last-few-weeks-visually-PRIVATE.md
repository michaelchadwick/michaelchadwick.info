Blogging with words doesn&#8217;t come as naturally to me anymore. I feel that the community once engendered by LiveJournal, of which I was a proud member and contributor for many years at one time, does not exist on the Open Internet. We are scattered all over the place, micro-bloggingly and macro-bloggingly. Comments are few and far between and there&#8217;s no cohesiveness to the whole thing.

The hardest thing is just letting go and blogging for the sake of blogging. When I know there&#8217;s a very small chance I will get any direct feedback, my mind wanders and my focus wanes. I imagine myself speaking into a dark, empty cave, hearing only myself and the boredom and tedium of my thoughts.

For now, I will simply post some pictures that both sum up and don&#8217;t sum up at all the last few weeks, which encompassed my birthday, Christmas, and New Year&#8217;s.
